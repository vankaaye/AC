Getting Started

Four easy steps. We'll hold your hand through all of them.

Aged care paperwork can feel like a maze. We do this every day — so you don't have to figure it out alone.

  1. 1

    Say hello

    Call us or pop in for a cuppa. We'll listen, answer everything, and help with My Aged Care and ACAT assessments.

  2. 2

    Plan together

    We design a care plan around your health, habits and happiness — and check it with you and your family first.

  3. 3

    Settle in gently

    Familiar furniture, favourite meals, a buddy from day one. Moving in (or starting home visits) made easy.

  4. 4

    We keep listening

    Care reviews every quarter and honest chats every day. As needs change, care changes too — smoothly.

Good Questions

The things families always ask us

No question is too small or too awkward. If it's not answered here, call — a real human picks up.

Call 1800 ASTRO
Will the government help pay for our care?

Almost always, yes. Most Australians over 65 qualify for subsidised care through My Aged Care after a free assessment. We'll help you arrange it and show you exactly what's covered — no jargon, no obligation.

Can we visit before we decide?

Please do! Tour any residence, stay for lunch, chat with residents. And once you're with us, family can visit every day of the year — meals, parties and sleepovers included.

What makes your dementia care special?

Purpose-designed spaces (circular paths, sensory gardens, no confusing corridors) and carers with specialist dementia training. We meet people where they are — keeping days calm and identities intact.

What if care needs grow over time?

Then care grows too. Because we offer everything from light home help to palliative care, changing needs mean a conversation — not another stressful move.

How fast can care start?

Emergency respite can often start within 48 hours. Home care usually starts within a week or two. For residential rooms we'll tell you honestly what's available the day you call.
