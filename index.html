The Astro Care Difference

Ageing deserves reverence, not routine.

Astro Care was founded on a simple conviction: the later chapters of life should be written with the same richness as the first. We are an Australian, family-minded aged care provider — small enough to know every resident's story, capable enough to meet every clinical need.

From our residences to our in-home care teams, everything is shaped around one question: what does a good day look like for you? Then we build the care around the answer.

  • Dignity first

    Choice, privacy and respect in every interaction — never care done to someone, always with them.

  • Clinical excellence

    Registered nurses around the clock, GP partnerships, and care plans reviewed with families — not filed away.

  • Family, always

    Open doors, honest conversations, and a seat at the table for the people who love you.

Your Journey With Us

Four gentle steps, one guided path.

Navigating aged care can feel overwhelming. We walk beside you from the first phone call — explaining funding, assessments and options in plain language.

  1. 01

    A Conversation

    Call us or visit for tea. We listen to your story, answer every question, and help with ACAT assessment and My Aged Care if you need it.

  2. 02

    A Personal Care Plan

    Our clinical team designs a plan around your health, habits, culture and preferences — reviewed with you and your family before anything begins.

  3. 03

    A Seamless Welcome

    Moving in or starting home visits, we handle the details. Familiar furniture, favourite meals, a friendly face assigned from day one.

  4. 04

    Care That Evolves

    Formal reviews every quarter and honest conversations every day. As needs change, care changes with them — never the other way around.

How does government funding work — and will we qualify?

Most Australians over 65 qualify for subsidised care through My Aged Care, after a free ACAT/ACAS assessment. We help you arrange the assessment, understand your Home Care Package level or residential funding, and see exactly what is subsidised versus private. No obligation, no jargon.

What makes your dementia care different?

Our memory-support wings are purpose-designed — circular walking paths, sensory gardens, no dead-end corridors — and every carer completes specialist dementia training. We practise validation over correction: we meet residents where they are, keeping days calm and identities intact.

Can we visit before deciding? Can family visit anytime after?

Yes, and yes. Tour any residence, stay for lunch, talk to residents without a chaperone. Once you're with us, our doors are open to family every day of the year — including for meals, celebrations and overnight stays by arrangement.

What happens if care needs increase over time?

Care plans are formally reviewed every quarter and adjusted any day they need to be. Because we provide everything from light home support through to palliative care, increasing needs mean a conversation — not another stressful move.

How quickly can care begin?

Emergency respite can often begin within 48 hours. Home care typically starts within one to two weeks of assessment; residential placement depends on suite availability, which we'll tell you honestly on the day you call.

Begin with a conversation, not a contract.

Tell us about the person you love. We'll tell you honestly how we can help — and if we're not the right fit, we'll point you to who is.
