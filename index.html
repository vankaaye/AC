How it works — in plain English

The government helps pay for care at home in two main ways. Don't worry about picking — call us and we'll work out which one suits you, then handle the paperwork together.

Option 1Home Care Package (HCP)

Best if you need regular or growing help — from a few visits a week to daily care.

  1. 1
    Call My Aged Care on 1800 200 422 — or call us first and we'll help you register. It takes about 10 minutes.
  2. 2
    Have a free assessment at home. A friendly assessor visits and works out your package level (1 to 4).
  3. 3
    Choose Astro Care as your provider. We plan your care with you and manage the budget openly.
  4. 4
    Your care begins. The same friendly faces, week after week — reviewed with you as needs change.

Option 2Commonwealth Home Support (CHSP)

Best if you need a little help to stay independent — like cleaning, meals or transport.

  1. 1
    Call us or My Aged Care. We'll check you're eligible (most people 65+ are).
  2. 2
    Have a quick phone or home chat with an assessor to agree on the services you'd like.
  3. 3
    Services start — often within days. You pay only a small contribution; the government covers the rest.

Feeling unsure? That's completely normal — the system confuses everyone. One call sorts it out.
